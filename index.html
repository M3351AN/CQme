Hello, I'm BG7QNF, a ham radio operator in Nanning, Guangxi, China.

TIP: When contacting me via mail or ONLINE (NOT QSO via radio, but via Internet), please feel free to use your native language.

Meeting on the air?

I am a QRP enthusiast and also a homebrew equipment(including but not limited to antennas/transceivers) enthusiast.

My QTH usually OL42, antenna altitude about 200m above sea level.

I usually operate at less than 5W PWR on 6m/2m/0.7m bands, and occasionally at slightly over 100W PWR on 15m, so I would be very surprised if you could successfully contact me at a distance.

At some time I will also try CW on 40m band with around 1W PWR.(but, currently 0 QSO)

About QSL cards

NOTE: You are welcome to send me QSL cards by postal mail. Eyeball QSO is also accepted. I promise to reply within 30 days.

No SASE or IRC required. But if you are a stamp collector, you can indicate that in your letter and I will be happy to exchange stamps and IRC with you.

Also accept eQSL, which you can send to my email or through eqsl.cc
